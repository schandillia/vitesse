## Filing a Grievance

You may submit your grievance via email. To help us address your concern promptly, please include the following details:

- Your full name and contact information
- A brief description of the grievance, including relevant dates and screenshots, if applicable
- The specific content, transaction, or issue involved
- The relief you seek

## Response Timeline

We acknowledge all grievances within 24 hours of receipt and aim to resolve them within 15 days from the date of acknowledgment, in accordance with applicable Indian laws. If we require more time, we will communicate the reason for the delay.
