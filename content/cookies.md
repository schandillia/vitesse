## What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website or use an app. They help us remember your preferences, improve performance, analyze usage, and offer personalized content.

## Types of Cookies We Use

We use the following categories of cookies:

- **Essential Cookies:** Necessary for the Service to function, including login authentication, session management, and secure checkout. These cannot be disabled.
- **Analytics Cookies:** Help us understand how users interact with the Service by collecting usage data. We use **PostHog**, which may place cookies on your device to collect aggregated traffic and usage information such as page views, time on site, and referrer information. [Learn more about PostHog’s data practices here](https://posthog.com/privacy).
- **Functional Cookies:** Remember your settings and preferences to enhance your experience.
- **Marketing Cookies:** May be used to deliver relevant advertisements and track their performance.

## Third-Party Cookies

Payment processors often use essential cookies to prevent fraud and securely process transactions. These third parties may collect data independently and are governed by their own privacy policies.

## Children and Cookie Usage

We do not knowingly use cookies or similar tracking technologies to collect personal information from users under the age of 13 (or under 16 in certain jurisdictions) without verifiable parental consent. We strive to comply with the requirements of the Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and the General Data Protection Regulation for Kids (GDPR-K) where applicable. For such users, marketing and personalized tracking cookies are disabled by default, and only essential cookies necessary for security and session management are enabled.
